1. Pending intervention types

What the proposer can queue for a sub-account. Each one becomes an actions row + a reviewItems card awaiting your one-click approval. Action type is client_pulse_intervention; the template slug (shown below each) controls trigger condition + message copy.

✉︎
dormant_staff_activity_checkin
Check-in email — "Everything okay?"
Trigger: Staff activity ≥14 days silent
Agency-branded email to the client asking a light-touch question. Non-threatening; designed to surface problems before cancellation.
▶︎
no_funnel_nurture
4-video funnel-setup nurture sequence
Trigger: 0 funnels built, client ≥30 days onboarded
4-day email drip teaching funnel-build basics; first funnel is the stickiest onboarding milestone.
📅
calendar_setup_guide
Calendar setup guide + optional VA-help offer
Trigger: Calendar quality below team-size ratio threshold
Step-by-step guide email; optional "reply YES to have our VA set it up for you" CTA.
📞
tier_downgrade_retention_alert
Retention-call task to VA
Trigger: Tier decreased in last 90 days (typical 90-day re-downgrade window)
Creates an internal task assigned to a VA; includes "offer 30-day DFY funnel audit" as the hook angle.
🤖
third_party_ai_upsell
AI comparison + upsell (native vs third-party)
Trigger: CloseBot or similar third-party AI detected
Comparison info for GHL's native AI Studio vs the third-party tool; usually framed as a consolidation-saves-money angle.
🎯
trial_milestone_nudge
Trial-milestone nudge (per incomplete step)
Trigger: Trial user stalled ≥72h on a milestone
Specific nudge per incomplete step (first funnel, first calendar, first campaign…). Reuses Kel's 25-email sequence.
📉
follow_up_overdue_deal
Follow-up task on stalled opportunity
Trigger: Opportunity ≥30 days in same stage, beyond cohort baseline
Creates operator task; win-rate drops 48% after 14 days at this stage — nudge fast.
💰
ad_campaign_optimization
Pause-or-pivot on underperforming ad
Trigger: CPL >2× target for 7 days
Recommends specific action: pause campaign, reallocate budget, or swap creative. Reads from Uphex/GHL ads data.
🔁
reactivation_offer
Win-back offer to churned contact
Trigger: Contact cancelled ≥90 days ago, hasn't been reached since
Reactivation email with discount or limited-time offer. Configurable incentive.
🛠️
workflow_fix_recommendation
Broken workflow / automation repair
Trigger: Workflow error rate spike detected by anomaly
Operator task with diagnostic details and a suggested fix; links directly to the workflow editor.
📈
tier_upgrade_recommendation
Upsell — usage supports a tier upgrade
Trigger: Contact/deal volume >50% over current tier cap for 30 days
Agency-branded email framing the upgrade around the client's demonstrated growth. Data-driven sales play.
🧹
integration_adoption_nudge
"You're not using integration X — here's why it matters"
Trigger: Integration connected but <5 events in last 30 days
Flags installed-but-dormant integrations. Frames adoption around a specific comparable client with similar profile.

3. Blind-spot patterns ("what your team might be forgetting")

Pattern-detection insights surfaced per sub-account. Each one is an implicit observation — the system looked across the account's data (plus the portfolio baseline) and noticed something nobody was tracking. Sourced from the anomaly detection skill + comparative cohort analysis.

📵
silent_channel
Silent channel (no outbound for N days)
"Last outbound SMS from your team to any Bright contact was Sarah, 8 days ago." Every other client of theirs with healthy retention has weekly staff-to-contact SMS. This client's silence is your silence.
Per-channel silence detection (email, SMS, phone, in-app). Compared against this client's historical cadence AND portfolio baseline.
⏰
unread_conversation_aging
Unread inbound conversation aging
"3 inbound messages from client contacts are sitting unread >24h." Longest: Maria @ Highland, sent Mon 09:14, marked seen but not replied.
Tracks unread / unanswered customer-side messages across all channels. Flags the oldest.
⏳
pipeline_stage_stall
Deal stalled past stage baseline
"Chavez Auto is 12 days in 'Proposal Sent' — your baseline for this stage is 6 days." Win rate halves after day 14.
Per-stage duration anomaly vs this client's historical stage-velocity baseline.
💳
failed_payment_unresolved
Payment failure not retried
"A $247 subscription charge failed 4 days ago and nobody's retried." Default retry cadence is 24h; this is silent past that.
Declined cards, failed renewals, bounced invoices that nobody's followed up on.
📭
campaign_performance_drift
Campaign performance quietly degrading
"Email open rate dropped from 28% to 19% over the last 3 sends." Subject-line drift or list fatigue; worth a diagnostic.
Campaign KPIs silently trending worse than the account's 30-day baseline.
🧑‍💼
team_capacity_bottleneck
Single staff member holds most overdue work
"Sarah owns 43% of overdue tasks for this client." Reassign or escalate — she's the bottleneck.
Detects workload concentration that predicts miss-deadlines.
🔌
feature_adoption_gap
Integration installed but dormant
"CloseBot is installed on Bright, but only 2 messages in 30 days." Either configure it properly or consider disconnecting.
Integration detection says it's there; usage intensity says it's not being used. Signal it.
🏁
renewal_unaddressed
Contract renewal approaching with no discussion
"Contract renewal window opens in 14 days — no renewal conversation found in the last 90 days."
Scans conversation history for renewal-keyword activity; flags silent approach.
🕵️
competitor_mention_detected
Competitor mentioned in client conversations
"'HubSpot' mentioned twice by Ben this week — might be evaluating alternatives."
Keyword scanning over conversation metadata. Configurable competitor watchlist per org.
🎰
multi_signal_churn_composite
Multi-signal composite risk emerged
"3 negative signals co-occurred this week: failed payment + 14d silence + stage regression." Any one alone is noise; together they're a churn signal.
Composite risk detection; only fires when ≥3 indicators cluster within a short window.
📉
cohort_deviation
Outlier vs comparable-client cohort
"Contact-create rate this week is 20% of the cohort average." 8 similar clients (same vertical, same tier) averaged 43 new contacts; Bright added 9.
Cross-client cohort comparison surfaces accounts lagging peers on key metrics.
🗂️
data_freshness_lag
Data sync stale / integration unhealthy
"GHL sync for this client hasn't run successfully in 36 hours." All signals based on stale data.
Operational blind spot — flags when the health score itself might be wrong because the connector isn't pulling.

4. Intervention outcome narratives

Schema-level outcome (improved | unchanged | worsened) plus a narrative category explaining why the health-score delta happened. Source: server/db/schema/interventionOutcomes.ts + the measurement loop that runs measurementWindowHours after action.completedAt.

✓
conversion_completed
Intervention → conversion event
Retention call → audit booked, +$4,200 deal closed within 7 days. Health +6.
The intervention correlates with a measurable revenue event. Strongest possible outcome.
📈
engagement_increased
Measurable engagement spike
Check-in email → Ben replied within 4h, 3 follow-up messages that day. Staff activity density doubled WoW. Health +3.
No deal yet but the account came back to life — message volume, activity, opens, clicks all spiked.
🛟
churn_prevented
Churn risk averted / band held
Forecast was Projected Critical by Wed; band held at At Risk (31). Forecast averted.
The intervention stopped a forecast band deterioration from materialising. Not a recovery, but a successful holding action.
🛠️
process_fixed
Process or workflow repaired
Broken workflow repair → error rate 12% → 0%, automation completed 1,840 contacts. Health +2.
Targeted operational fix (workflow, funnel, calendar, integration). Immediate observable improvement.
→
no_effect_observed
Executed, no downstream signal
Nurture sequence sent to 1,240 contacts. 24% open / 3.2% click. No band movement, no engagement spike.
The action shipped but didn't correlate with any observable change in the account. Common; worth tracking to refine the template.
📭
no_response
No response from client
Check-in email sent Mon. 0 opens, 0 replies, 0 subsequent activity. Switch to a different channel next cycle.
Specific sub-case of "no effect" where the client didn't engage at all. Signals to try a different channel or copy angle.
✗
negative_signal
Accelerated decline post-intervention
Tier-upgrade upsell → client immediately downgraded from $647 to $147 two days later. Wrong timing.
Rare and valuable — the intervention may have actively harmed the relationship. Triggers a pattern-learning entry so the proposer avoids this trigger condition in future.
⏳
insufficient_measurement_window
Outcome deferred — not enough time
Funnel nurture sequence is 4 days in; measurement window closes at 14 days. Check back Fri next week.
Some interventions (long drip sequences, retention tasks) have slower feedback loops. Outcome row is created as "pending" and finalised later.
🎓
pattern_candidate
Successful outcome → template promotion candidate
"Funnel-refresh with hero + social proof moved conversion 1.8% → 3.1% in 3 days." Second similar lift this month — Orchestrator flags it as a portfolio-wide template candidate.
When an outcome pattern recurs across clients, the intervention template graduates from one-off to library-grade.
🛑
halted_by_operator
Operator rejected the proposal before execution
Tier-upgrade upsell proposal rejected — "Client's main contact is out on leave this month." Cooldown extended.
Rejection is itself a valuable signal. The rejection comment gets stored + weighted into future proposer behaviour for that account.

5. Memory block tag types ("what the system learned this week")

Memory blocks drive what the system remembers about a sub-account / client. They come from two sources — manual (human-authored) and auto_synthesised (system-generated from clustered observations). Four status states: active, draft, pending_review, rejected. Schema: server/db/schema/memoryBlocks.ts.

Belief Ben (Bright Marketing, owner) responds to text, not email.
Discrete fact with a confidence score. Influences future intervention proposals for this account (e.g. proposer picks SMS over email). Source: manual or auto_synthesised from ≥3 citations.
Pattern Funnel refresh (hero + social proof) moved conversion 1.8% → 3.1% in 3 days.
Recurring behavioural signal derived from ≥5 entries. If a similar lift appears for 2 more clients, Orchestrator proposes it as a standard intervention template portfolio-wide.
Cooldown Next retention-touch not permitted until Tue 9 May (18-day cooldown on retention-call interventions).
Intervention suppression state. Prevents the proposer from repeatedly hammering the same trigger.
Correction Earlier belief "$347 tier is Bright's growth tier" is wrong — their plan budget is $147; $347 was a one-month promo.
Human override that invalidates a prior belief. The old belief is soft-deleted but kept for audit.
Relationship Ben is the decision-maker at Bright; Sarah (account manager) is the day-to-day contact; Chris (admin) handles billing.
Contact hierarchy mapping. Influences who receives which type of agency-branded outreach.
Preference Bright prefers weekly digests delivered Thursday, not Friday (Ben's team does week-in-review on Thu).
Stylistic or process choice. Overrides defaults — the per-client digest schedule gets adjusted.
Constraint This client has a no-automated-outbound policy; all agency-branded messages require explicit per-send approval.
Hard operational limitation. Blocks the Operate-tier proposer from auto-sending even with an approval; forces HITL on every message.
Decision Paused paid ads for Bright in March; switching to organic funnel strategy for Q2.
Historical choice with rationale. Audit-trail entry so next quarter's review has context.
Opportunity Bright only monitors one location; they actually have three storefronts — 2 un-onboarded. Upsell candidate.
Unmet capability / expansion signal. Gets surfaced to the operator as a growth-play candidate.
Risk Support response time degraded to 8h avg; was 4h. Trending worse for 3 weeks.
Anomaly-driven threshold breach. Worth surfacing even if the account's band hasn't dropped yet — leading indicator.
Insight Bright's email campaigns with subject-line <10 chars convert 40% higher than their typical 30-char lines.
Non-obvious correlation found by the synthesis step. Gets shown in the digest; can be cited by the proposer.
Hypothesis Bright's recent CPL spike may correlate with the Meta algorithm change on 8 April — unverified; check again next week.
Tentative explanation awaiting more evidence. Shown in digest with explicit confidence marker. Promoted to insight or pattern once validated.
Confidence-tier routing (from memory-and-briefings spec §5.7): auto-synthesised blocks with confidence >0.85 → active immediately; 0.6–0.85 → pending_review (HITL queue); <0.6 → dropped. Manual blocks always start active unless marked draft.

Every action an agent can invoke. These are the capabilities beneath everything in Sections 1–12 — most don't surface as a visible UI item, they underpin the system's ability to do work on behalf of a sub-account. Source: server/config/actionRegistry.ts (50+ entries, each with actionType, gateLevel, idempotencyStrategy, scopeRequirements). Items flagged [spec only] exist in design docs but aren't yet implemented. Items flagged [gated] require human approval before execution.

📱

Tiering note (per decision D6): the read-only intelligence + information-retrieval + system actions are available on the Monitor tier (~$1,200/mo anchor). The write / execute / propose actions — especially send_email, send_sms, create_page, publish_page, trigger_account_intervention, create_event, create_deal — unlock on the Operate tier (~$2,400/mo anchor). Monitor-tier orgs see health + risk + blind spots; Operate-tier orgs get the automated execution on top.
