- [P2] Keep fallback cron from skipping pending baselines — C:/Files/Projects/automation-v1/tasks/builds/stream-1-onboarding-scope/plan.md:132-132

Narrow this guard to terminal already-handled states such as `captured`/`manual`, or apply it only when creating the initial pending row.
