Subaccount Blueprints

Reusable agent-hierarchy setups for your subaccounts. When you create a new subaccount, the default blueprint is applied — its agents, links, schedules, and skill assignments are seeded automatically. Pick from a system-admin-published Organisation Template to bootstrap, or author your own from scratch.

💡

What changed in Phase A

Blueprints used to also carry your org-level operational config (scoring weights, churn bands, intervention defaults). After the platform cleanup, those settings live on the organisation itself — edit them under ClientPulse Settings or via the Configuration Assistant. Blueprints are now strictly about agent hierarchy + which operational defaults to seed when a new subaccount is created.

Mental model. Three levels of templating, all separate after the Phase A cleanup:
① Organisation Template (system admin) — defines the archetype of an org. Adopted once at signup.
② Subaccount Blueprint (this page) — defines how a new subaccount's agents are wired up. Per-org, reusable.
③ ClientPulse Settings (org admin) — the runtime operational config (weights, bands, intervention defaults, alert limits) that applies across all subaccounts in your org. Edited via typed editors or the Configuration Assistant.
