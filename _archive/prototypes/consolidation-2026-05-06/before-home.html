Problems with current state (basis for Round 6 redesign)

  • 4 KPI tiles at top (Pending Approval, Clients Needing Attention, Active Agents, Runs 7 days) expose internal metrics. Operators need to act, not monitor numbers.
  • "Pending your approval" section duplicates Inbox content. Items appear in both places, creating confusion about where to act.
  • OperationalMetricsPlaceholder renders as an empty visible gap in the layout (LAYOUT-RESERVED comment in source, component not implemented yet).
  • "Your workspaces" section is a weak nav shortcut to ClientPulse and Settings, both already reachable from the sidebar.
  • FreshnessIndicator adds visual noise. WebSocket keeps data live; showing "updated X seconds ago" adds anxiety, not clarity.
  • No primary "New agent" action anywhere on the page. The dashboard has no clear call to action.
  • AgentRecommendationsList section has jarring conditional visibility: hidden on first load, appears after data fetch, shifts layout.
  • No glanceable view of run activity or active agents. The operator cannot tell "is anything running right now?" without reading the activity feed.
