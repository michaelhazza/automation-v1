What changed from v1

  • Plain language only. No category slugs (no agent.over_budget, no memory.low_citation_waste). The operator never sees what the engine calls a finding internally.
  • Progressive disclosure. Top 3 only. The other 5 are one click away if the operator wants them — most won't.
  • One action per row. "Help me fix this →" is the primary path. The small × is the only secondary action. Acknowledge / category tags / timestamps removed — they were noise.
  • Numbers in human terms. "$73 vs $50 budget", "12 seconds vs 3", "8 of the last 12". No p95s, no percentages of percentages.
  • Severity is a single dot. Red / amber / grey. The ordering does most of the work; the dot is a quick visual cue, not a label.
