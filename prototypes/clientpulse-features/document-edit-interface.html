1. Logo
A
Apex Window Cleaning
The Apex wordmark should always be presented in the primary indigo or white on dark backgrounds. The mark and wordmark must maintain a minimum clear space of 1× the mark height on all sides. Never stretch, rotate, or add effects to the logo.

4. Photography Style
Photography should convey professionalism, cleanliness, and a sense of pride in craft. Preferred shots: crew at work in natural daylight, before-and-after window comparisons, satisfied homeowners (with consent). Avoid stock photos featuring generic cleaning scenarios unrelated to window cleaning.
Approved: natural light, real crew
Avoid: staged stock photos
5. Voice and Tone
Apex's brand voice is warm, reliable, and quietly confident. We never oversell. Copy should feel like a conversation with a trusted neighbour, not a sales pitch. Use active verbs. Keep sentences short. Avoid jargon.
Do: "We'll take care of it" · "Book in under 2 minutes" · "Streak-free, guaranteed"
Don't: "Utilise our premium end-to-end window-cleaning solutions" · All-caps for emphasis
