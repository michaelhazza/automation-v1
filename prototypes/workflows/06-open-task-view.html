Acme renewal proposal · Draft v1 · authored by Head of Sales · 28s ago

Acme — Q3 Renewal Proposal

Acme has been a customer since Q2 2024. Over the past four quarters they've moved from initial-pilot tier to mid-market tier, with $48K ARR and 23% NRR expansion driven primarily by additional seats and the upgrade to the Pro Workflows package in February.

Renewal terms

We propose extending Acme's current contract for a further twelve months at a 9% list-price increase, offset by a 3% loyalty credit and twelve months of priority support. Net effect on Acme's invoice: a 6% increase, in line with their internal 7% benchmark for mission-critical SaaS.

Why we believe this works

  • Acme's usage has grown 23% YoY; pricing should reflect realised value.
  • Competitor pricing (see annex) sits ~12% above our proposed renewal.
  • Our minimum-margin policy (15% on renewals) is comfortably met at this rate.

What we're asking for

Mike's sign-off on the body of this proposal and the pricing annex (separate document). On approval, the COO consolidates and the SDR sends through the Acme primary contact in GHL.
