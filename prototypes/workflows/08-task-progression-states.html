State 1 · Just kicked off. The thinking box reads "Drafting the plan." Activity has 2-3 events. Plan tab shows the steps as forming (current border). Notice: the chat has only the operator's prompt and one orchestrator response. No per-agent log on the left, that's what activity is for.
State 2 · Multi-agent working. The thinking box reads "Working with Sales, Research, Operations · 3 agents active." Activity is pumping events from the three sub-agents. Now tab shows the org chart with three sub-agents' status dots pulsing blue. The chat hasn't grown, the conversation is still just the operator's prompt and the orchestrator's milestone.
State 3 · Fetching references. The thinking box reads "Fetching competitor rate cards." Activity shows the Stripe and PayPal fetches with linked entity chips. Files tab is the natural focus, with the rate cards landing on the references side of the divider; the Stripe PDF is selected and rendered in the reader.
State 4 · File created. The thinking box reads "Reviewing the draft." Activity surfaces the new draft with a chip back to it. Chat gets a milestone card from Head of Sales speaking directly ("Body drafted ✓ Pulled SDR-1 for last quarter MRR + terms.") with a "Open draft" link. The sub-agent owns the milestone, not the orchestrator narrating it. Files tab auto-selects the freshly produced Acme proposal and renders it in the reader.
State 5 · Awaiting approval. The thinking box reads "Waiting on your approval." Activity records the approval-queued event. Chat now has the full milestone arc: each sub-agent (Sales, Research, Operations) speaks once with their deliverable + a link, then COO consolidates and asks for approval with inline Approve / Open files buttons. This is the v2 milestone-shaped pattern: every card in chat is a state-change worth surfacing. Plan tab highlights step 5 (the Approval) with a confidence chip ("Medium · the next step can't be undone, worth a careful look") and shows the Critical pill on step 6 (Send to Acme). When Mike clicks Approve, the audit fields snapshot what he saw at this exact moment.

All five states render from a single WebSocket subscription on the open task. Each pane filters and renders the events relevant to it. No polling, no per-pane fetches. The thinking box is a derived field on the latest event; the activity list is the event stream verbatim; the right pane is whichever tab the operator has selected, redrawing on relevant events.
