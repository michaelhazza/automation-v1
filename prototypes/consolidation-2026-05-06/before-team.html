Problems with current state

  • Two separate pages for related data: members list at the subaccount level, org chart at the org level. No single place to see both.
  • Org chart is read-only. Reporting structure cannot be edited inline. Requires engineering change to update.
  • No way to see a member's reporting line from the member list. Must navigate to a different route to see context.
  • Reaching the org chart from a subaccount context requires switching to org-level mode, breaking the user's mental context.
  • Adding a new member (from SubaccountTeamPage) does not prompt the admin to place that person in the org chart.
  • Org chart cards show only name and role. No email, no avatar, no inline action. Cannot click through to member detail.
  • Member detail editing (role, permission set, status) requires a separate modal with no context about the member's position or activity.
See the consolidated after state: team.html (Members + Org Chart tabs, member detail drawer, invite modal)
