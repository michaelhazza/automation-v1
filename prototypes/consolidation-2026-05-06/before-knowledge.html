These are the pages being replaced. Shown as faithful approximations, not exact pixel copies. The goal is to make the delta visible.

Problems with the before state: (1) SubaccountKnowledgePage used "Promote" button terminology unknown to non-technical operators. (2) WorkspaceMemoryPage exposed schema concepts ("blocks", block types, suppress, re-add) directly in the UI. (3) MemoryReviewQueuePage surfaced as a separate route, breaking the operator's context. (4) Schema concepts leaked into UI: "blocks", "promote", "suppress", "re-add", "re-suppress" are engineering terms that non-technical operators could not decode. (5) Review Queue was a separate page rather than an inline state. (6) No clear distinction between operator-authored knowledge and agent-extracted observations.
