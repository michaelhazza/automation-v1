Professional but warm. Avoid jargon and sports metaphors. Use "we" when referring to the agency. Lead with outcomes before features. Max 3 paragraphs per outbound email. Never use passive voice in subject lines.

Operating constraints — compliance
No cold outreach before 8am or after 6pm local time. HITL response SLA: 2 business hours. Commitments above $10k require VP sign-off. GDPR: no PII stored outside EU data residency zone without consent.

Proof library — case studies
TechBridge (SaaS, 3x lead velocity), Meridian Health (compliance automation, 60% SLA improvement), Logan Capital (reporting automation, 14 hrs/week saved). Use the "Before/After" frame when presenting in discovery calls.

Operating procedures — escalation policy
Tier 1: agent self-resolve. Tier 2: flag in Inbox for operator review within 2h. Tier 3: page on-call lead (PagerDuty). Client-facing escalations always copied to Account Executive. Never escalate directly to engineering.
