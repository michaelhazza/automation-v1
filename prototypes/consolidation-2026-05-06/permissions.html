Permission Patterns

How to communicate permission boundaries in the UI. Use these patterns consistently across all pages. Builder reference, not a real page.

Admin-only content is dashed-amber bordered and labelled, not hidden. It should be visible to non-admins but clearly scoped.
Locked rows are greyed, not removed. Operators should know the item exists, just not be able to act on it.
Gated action buttons are greyed out with a lock icon and a tooltip explaining the required role. They are never invisible.
"You don't have access" full-screen states are only for routes that are entirely inaccessible (not just partially restricted).
