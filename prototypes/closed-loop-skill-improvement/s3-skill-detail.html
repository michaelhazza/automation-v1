Example From failed run
Missed deadline apology: open with direct acknowledgement before any explanation.
Accepted 13 May 2026
Improvement text
When a deadline has been missed, open the email with a direct, empathetic acknowledgement before any other content. Example: "Dear [Name], I want to address directly that we did not deliver [deliverable] by [date]. I am sorry for the disruption this caused. Here is our plan to make it right: ..." Do not lead with explanations or context before the apology.
Instruction From failed run
Always include a "Next steps" section as a numbered list in any action-requiring email.
Accepted 8 May 2026
Improvement text
When the email requires any action from the recipient, include a "Next steps" section formatted as a numbered list. Place it immediately before the sign-off. Each step should be a single sentence with a clear owner and due date where known.
Guardrail
Never include the client's internal Salesforce ID in any external-facing email.
Added 2 May 2026
Improvement text
Do not include Salesforce record IDs, opportunity IDs, or any internal CRM identifiers in emails addressed to external recipients. This includes body text, subject lines, and attachments. Reference contacts and companies by name only.
