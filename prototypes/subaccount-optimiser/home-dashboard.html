What's actually changing on this page

  • Only one new thing. The "A few things to look at" section sits between "Pending your approval" and "Your workspaces". Everything else on the page is exactly what's there today.
  • Why here? This is the agency operator's actual home (/). Not the Pulse dashboard. Not a new nav item. The operator already lands here in the morning, so suggestions surface where they already are.
  • Pulse is no longer the home for cross-client suggestions. Pulse is for portfolio health (a different view of clients). Suggestions could later get a small widget on Pulse too if the org uses it, but the home dashboard is the primary surface.
  • Section is hidden when there's nothing to show. Zero open suggestions = the section disappears entirely. No empty-state clutter.
  • "See all 12 →" link takes the operator to a full /suggestions list page (deferred — not part of this v3 mock; the section + "see all" pattern is enough for v1).
