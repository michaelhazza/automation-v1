Designer notes — review-queue-green.html
Extends: client/src/pages/ReviewQueuePage.tsx — header block, tab group, queue item cards, approve/edit/reject actions. Also references client/src/pages/operate/InboxPage.tsx for the three-band list pattern. Composite header is a NEW component injected above the tab group.

Green state: callout is informational ("All systems healthy") and subdued. No amber/red attention styles. Sparkline shows 30-day upward trend. Details link opens drill-in dialog. "Refresh now" is a low-emphasis text link beneath the details button.
Principles: (1) workflow-anchored: operator opens review queue daily to act; (2) default-to-hidden: composite header is minimal — just number, band, trend, callout, one action; (3) one primary action: approve/reject items; (4) inline state: composite communicates health without leaving the page; (5) re-check: non-technical operator sees a green score and a list of items to review.
