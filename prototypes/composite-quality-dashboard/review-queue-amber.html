Designer notes — review-queue-amber.html
Extends: client/src/pages/ReviewQueuePage.tsx — same header/tab/item structure. Composite header shows amber band pill, down arrow, attention callout. Overdue items are visually separated (amber left-border, overdue-badge) and float to the top per brief §3.3 attention callout priority rule 2. The queue is NOT reordered arbitrarily — overdue items surface because that is exactly the callout ("5 amendments awaiting review for over 24 hours").
Sparkline uses amber colour (fde68a) and shows a downward 30-day trajectory. Callout text is bold amber (attention class). "Details" opens the drill-in dialog (review-queue-drill-in-dimensions.html).
Principles: (1) workflow-anchored: yes; (2) default-to-hidden: yes, no extra panels; (3) one primary action: approve/reject; (4) inline state: amber border + overdue badge tells the story without a separate dashboard; (5) re-check: operator sees 5 overdue items highlighted, knows exactly what to do first.
