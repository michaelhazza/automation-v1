Designer notes — review-queue-red.html
Extends: client/src/pages/ReviewQueuePage.tsx and client/src/pages/operate/InboxPage.tsx (HIGH PRIORITY band concept).
Key decision: safety banner appears ABOVE the composite header (separate component), not inside it. Per brief §3.2 and §8: "Safety regressions surface as red banners that override the composite presentation. The composite is supplementary; safety is primary." The safety item is also pinned to the top of the queue list above the overdue section. Composite header still shows (52, red, -15) so the operator has the full picture, but the safety callout drives the immediate action.
Sparkline shows sharp decline (red colour, steeper slope). Attention callout is bold red and names the specific skill slug (pii_redaction_v2). The "Jump to item" link anchors to the safety item within the page.
Principles: (1) workflow-anchored: yes; (2) default-to-hidden: safety banner is only shown when active; (3) one primary action: Review the safety item; (4) inline state: red composite + safety banner communicate urgency without a separate page; (5) re-check: non-technical operator cannot miss the red safety banner and the pinned safety item.
