Designer notes — home-with-chip-red.html
Extends: client/src/pages/operate/HomePage.tsx. Key design decision: the safety banner appears ABOVE the KPI grid (not inside the chip tile). Per brief §3.2, "safety-class metrics live outside the composite." The banner is a separate component from the composite chip — it would appear even if composite were green. Chip uses 2px red border (fca5a5). Bar chart shows a worsening failure trend over 14 days, consistent with a red composite state. Run activity chart (RunActivityChart) informs the bars' shape.
Principles: (1) workflow-anchored: yes, safety banner has a direct "Go to review queue" CTA; (2) default-to-hidden: safety banner is shown only when a safety regression is active; (3) one primary action: Go to review queue; (4) inline state: chip + safety banner communicate full picture; (5) re-check: non-technical operator sees the red banner first, knows action is needed, clicks through.
