What to sync on first connection

Currently open tickets and recent messages

Pulls all open tickets plus their last 30 days of message history. The agent can start working immediately. Recommended for most teams.

Open tickets only

Pulls ticket headers without message history. Faster initial sync; the agent will fetch message threads on demand as it works each ticket.

Historical backfill (months or years of closed tickets) is not included here. Training data lives in Foundry, not the live support agent.
