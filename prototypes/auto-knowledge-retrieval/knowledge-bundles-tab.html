Documents Bundles
i
Bundles are groupings, not loading rules
A bundle is a curatorial collection of documents you can attach to an agent or task in one action. Each document inside a bundle still has its own loading mode (Auto, Always available, Reference only) and is loaded into context per its own rules. Smart loading applies per-document, regardless of bundle.

When to use a bundle: Bundles are useful when you have a logical group of documents you want to attach to multiple agents at once (compliance kits, product packs, etc.) or when you want to clone a curated collection across sub-accounts. The smart loading model still decides what gets loaded per task.
