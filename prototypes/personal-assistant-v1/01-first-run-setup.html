Voice profile
Your assistant will draft emails and messages in your voice — not a generic AI voice.
We'll read your last 50 sent emails from the past 90 days and learn your writing style — greeting patterns, sentence length, signoff, signature. The result is a feature-level profile (greeting / signoff / register / sentence stats). We do not store the original email content, and your voice profile is private to you.
Sample size:50 messages
